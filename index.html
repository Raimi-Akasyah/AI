Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the programme.

Which subjects can I integrate AI tools like Copilot into?
▾
These tools are cross-curricular. You can use Copilot for lesson planning in any subject and use Reading, Math, and Speaker Progress to personalise assessment and feedback in literacy, numeracy, and public speaking across all relevant subjects.
Do students need their own Microsoft accounts to use the Learning Accelerators?
▾
Students will need a Microsoft account, typically provided by the school. Your IT administrator can set up student accounts through Microsoft Education. Contact your school's IT department or reach out to us for guidance.
How do I handle students without reliable internet access?
▾
Many AI tools offer offline or low-bandwidth modes. We also provide guidance on blended approaches that work even with limited connectivity. Reach out to your programme coordinator for location-specific support.
Is there a standardised lesson plan or curriculum guide available?
▾
Yes. Participants receive a full set of ready-to-use lesson plan templates, curriculum alignment guides, and educator handbooks upon programme registration. These are updated regularly to reflect the latest tools.
How can I access the online training modules again after my session?
▾
All online learning modules remain accessible to registered participants via our learning portal. You can revisit any module at any time through your personal account dashboard.
What is my role in the LEAPS Journey after training?
▾
After your Training of Trainers (ToT) session, you are expected to implement AI-enhanced lessons in your classroom, submit activity logs, and guide your students through the LEAPS milestones during the classroom implementation phase.
Am I eligible for the AI Training Hub programmes?
▾
Yes — the AI Training Hub is open to Malaysian students at all levels, from secondary school to university. Some programmes are also open to recent graduates entering the workforce. Check the specific programme page for eligibility details.
Are the courses free for students?
▾
Some programmes are fully funded through our partners (AVPN, Google.org, Yayasan Peneraju). Others offer subsidised rates. Contact us directly to find out which funded options you qualify for.
Will I receive a certificate upon completion?
▾
Yes. All participants who successfully complete their programme receive a Pepper Labs certificate. Certain tracks also carry HRD Corp claimable status or are recognised by partner universities and employers.
What is the time commitment for the upskilling track?
▾
The AI Upskilling Track runs for 4–8 weeks depending on the pathway. Sessions are typically scheduled on evenings or weekends to accommodate students with existing commitments. Self-paced options are also available.
